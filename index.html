What We Do

ScreenHub is an all inclusive software as a service (SaaS) content and design management system for your digital signs. Our web-based solution allows you to easily create, control, and modify messages on your sign from any internet enabled device. ScreenHub is hardware agnostic, making it compatible with any display type and screen resolution. We are based in the San Francisco Bay Area and have over 30 years of combined experience in Digital Signage, Software Development, Electrical Engineering, and Digital Marketing.
